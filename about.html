About Medication Reminder App

The Medication Reminder App is designed to help users stay on track with their medication schedules. Whether you’re managing a chronic condition, recovering from an illness, or just taking supplements, this app provides daily reminders so you never miss a dose.

Features include:

  • Daily pill reminders with customizable time
  • Calendar view to monitor your adherence
  • Reminder history tracking
  • Tablet usage information

This app is lightweight, simple to use, and fully runs in your browser using local storage—no data is shared or stored online.
